---- What We Offer ----

Lawn Service:

Lawn care is our specialty and main area of focus. When we come to your property, we include the following:

1: Picking up any toys/items on the lawn
2: Trimming with a line trimmer around all trees, fences, curbs, etc.
3: Edging with a metal blade edger
4: Mowing
5: Cleaning all clippings and debris

Price is determined based on the total area of grass. Mowing only is also available at discounted rates.
